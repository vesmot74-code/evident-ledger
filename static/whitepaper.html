Evident Ledger

Technical Whitepaper — Cryptographic Evidence Preservation Infrastructure
Version 1.0 · July 2026

Evident Ledger provides a vendor-independent, cryptographically verifiable method for preserving and validating the integrity and chronology of digital evidence — built for legal teams, forensic investigators, compliance officers, and auditors.

The whitepaper covers:

  • Evidence preservation model and cryptographic architecture (SHA-256, Ed25519, Merkle Trees, RFC3161 TSA)
  • Independent, offline verification without vendor participation
  • Authentication considerations under Federal Rules of Evidence 902(13) / 902(14)
  • System limitations and legal evidence considerations
